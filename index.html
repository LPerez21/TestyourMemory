How to Play!

Objective:

  • Find all matching pairs of cards in the shortest amount of time!

Gameplay:

  • Click to flip any two cards.
  • If the two cards match (same Peanuts character), they stay face up.
  • If the two cards don’t match, they are flipped back face down after a brief moment.
  • You must try to remember the location of cards as you flip them.
  • Matched cards will remain visible, helping you keep track of your progress.
  • Continue flipping cards until all pairs have been matched.

Good Luck!!
